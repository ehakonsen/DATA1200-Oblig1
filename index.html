Welcome to Bella Roma Italian restaurant!

Our cosy restaurant is located in the heart of trendy Grünerløkka. We pride ourselves on preparing tasty Italian meals made with fresh ingredients from authentic family recipes. Why not pop in for a takeaway snack, or perhaps enjoy an evening meal with friends?

We are open 13:00 to 22:00 seven days a week.

We look forward to seeing you soon!
