Welcome to Bella Roma Italian restaurant!

Our cosy restaurant is located in the heart of trendy Grünerløkka. We pride ourselves on preparing tasty Italian meals, using fresh ingredients and our own family recipes. We have plenty of takeaway snacks on offer for busy people on the go. If you prefer a more leisurely dining experience, we heartily recommend our three-course menu.

We are open 13:00 to 22:00 seven days a week.

We look forward to seeing you soon!
